## Warmup
- Bike or row 5 min @ easy
- World's greatest stretch 2x5 each side
- Goblet squat 2x8 @ light

## Main
- Back squat 4x5 @ RPE 7-8
- Romanian deadlift 3x8 @ RPE 7
- Bench press 4x5 @ RPE 7-8
- Chin-up 3x6 @ bodyweight; add load if easy
- Hanging knee raise 3x10

## Cooldown
- Couch stretch 2x60s each side
- Box breathing 5 min
