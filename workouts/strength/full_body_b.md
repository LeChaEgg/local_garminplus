## Warmup
- Row 5 min @ easy
- Hip airplane 2x5 each side
- Kettlebell deadlift 2x8 @ light

## Main
- Trap-bar deadlift 4x4 @ RPE 7-8
- Front squat 3x6 @ RPE 7
- Overhead press 4x5 @ RPE 7-8
- Pendlay row 3x6 @ RPE 7
- Farmer carry 3x40m @ heavy
- Hollow body hold 3x30s

## Cooldown
- Hip flexor stretch 2x60s each side
- Diaphragmatic breathing 5 min
